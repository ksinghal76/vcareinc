More Information

A Deal is a discount coupon that can be added to a listing and becomes active once associated to a listing. You begin by selecting the deals link and add your deal. To activate it choose a listing in the dropdown above and hit save, this will attach your deal to your listing. You can also click on the "gift box" icon within the listing section and this will attach your deal to your listing.

 
Your deal will show up at the directory high traffic areas:
  • -Within the deal section, there is an area where potential customers will find current deals. Making an initial search easy.
  • -Within the listing section, linked to a diamond listing you will see a clickable "deal icon". This lets visitors know that your listing is currently offering a promotional coupon.
